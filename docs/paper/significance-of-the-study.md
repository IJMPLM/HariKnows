# Significance of the Study

## Students
Students benefit from faster answers, clearer document guidance, and the ability to monitor their own requests without needing to repeatedly visit the office for routine questions.

## Registrar Staff
Registrar staff benefit from a more organized workflow for document tracking, FAQ maintenance, student credential updates, and bulk CSV handling.

## University Administration
The university benefits from a more consistent support experience, better record visibility, and a platform that reinforces privacy and accountability.

## Information Technology Staff
IT staff gain a structured system with clear data flows, database-backed operations, and a defined foundation for future improvements or integrations.

## Research Value
The project demonstrates how an AI-assisted helpdesk can be constrained by real institutional policy, database validation, and role-based access instead of relying on unconstrained chatbot responses.
