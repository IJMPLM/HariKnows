# Objectives of the Study

## General Objective
To design and document an AI-powered registrar helpdesk and tracking platform that improves student support, account management, and request visibility while preserving privacy and registrar control.

## Specific Objectives
- Provide round-the-clock registrar guidance for common student questions.
- Allow signed-in students to view and manage their own account-aware information.
- Support request tracking for registrar documents using the system status lifecycle.
- Centralize registrar FAQ and policy context into a structured knowledge base.
- Enable registrar staff to manage student credentials from existing student records.
- Support bulk CSV uploads for academic and account data with validation.
- Preserve privacy by limiting access to account-specific and sensitive information.
